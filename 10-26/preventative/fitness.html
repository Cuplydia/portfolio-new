Fitness

Our experienced staff of trainers can design the right "Fitness Program" to avert and avoid disease by maintaining a healthy lifestyle.

Fitness Plan 1-Let's Walk

Trainers at New Century can create a program to monitor your heart and steps, while you enjoy a walk or run in our one-eighth mile indoor walking and running track!. The indoor track is a great way to get in a walking or running workout away from the weather without having to resort to using a treadmill.

Fitness Plan 2-The waters fine

If you prefer the water, our Trainers can create a fitness program for patients who prefer exercise with low impact. New Century offers a state of the art Aquatic Center with an eight-lane, 25-yard, heated, year-round indoor pool. The Aquatic Center offers lap swimming, Aqua Aerobics and Zumba Aerobics.

Fitness Plan 3-Prefer the outdoors

Pickleball is a racket/paddle sport that was created by combining elements of several other racket sports. Sign up for a game of Pickleball our New Century's outdoor courts.
